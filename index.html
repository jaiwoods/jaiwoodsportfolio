Where Economics Meets Geography

Hello! I'm a fourth-year student soon to graduate from UC Santa Barbara double majoring in Economics and Geography with an emphasis in GIS. My work sits at the intersection of spatial analysis, economic systems, and emerging technology.

As a GIS analyst with experience in remote sensing, LiDAR data, and spatial modeling, I am fascinated by how spatial thinking can be applied to businesses for improved efficiency, growth, and site selection. I'm currently studying for the FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot License and have hands-on experience operating drones for research and content creation.

I'm exploring how geospatial intelligence, real estate analytics, and drone technology converge, and I'm looking for opportunities to put that to work. If you need a real estate analyst who also understands the spatial dimension of investment as well as the financial side, I am the perfect candidate for your role.
